Sherlock

Sherlock is an open-source intelligence (OSINT) tool designed for online investigations. It allows users to search for and gather information from various online platforms and social media networks based on a specific username or profile name. Sherlock is particularly useful for online reconnaissance, digital footprint analysis, and information gathering about individuals across the internet. The displayed table on the webpage represents profiles collected by Sherlock during its online investigations. These profiles include data such as usernames, platform names, profile links, HTTP status codes, and response times, all of which are collected from various online sources and platforms. The table provides a structured and organized view of the information gathered by Sherlock, making it easier for users to access and analyze the collected data.
